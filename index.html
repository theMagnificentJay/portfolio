ABOUT ME

I sit in the dark, 50% of my time is probably spent at my computer, I like my coffee black just like my metal.

If it doesn't come in black or have a darkmode, I'm not interested.

Hello, I am a computer enthusiast. Have been digging around and building them since my early pre-teens, from there I continued to develop a long lasting - and ever growing - hobby.

Continuing into my adulthood I transitioned from civilian life and into the military. With a heftier paycheck, I continued to develop skills and with the better pay I started building custom personal computers. Once I finished my main rig I wanted more - but what point is there in having more than one rig? I showed my friends and some of them really liked what I had, and since they were still using personal laptops I saw a golden opportunity. I started building PC's for my buddies as long as they provided the budget.

After a while I realized this was a good way to bring in some extra cash flow. I started charging roughly $100 USD - depending on the difficulty of the build - and really it just took off from there.

I transitioned back to civilian life in 2016 and now I am here back on the virtual for as long as I can be, attempting to become a better me, for me.
